Study Roadmap

Strategic preparation plan for NEET/JEE success

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

Physics

  • Basic Mechanics and Kinematics
  • Laws of Motion and Work-Energy
  • Properties of Matter and Fluids

Chemistry

  • Atomic Structure and Periodic Table
  • Chemical Bonding and States of Matter
  • Basic Organic Chemistry Concepts

Biology

  • Cell Structure and Functions
  • Biological Classification
  • Plant and Animal Tissues

Key Milestones:

  • NCERT textbook completion for covered topics
  • Weekly topic tests with 70% target accuracy
  • Foundation concept clarity assessment

Phase 2: Building Strength (Months 4-6)

Physics

  • Thermodynamics and Waves
  • Optics and Modern Physics
  • Electrostatics and Current Electricity

Chemistry

  • Chemical Equilibrium and Kinetics
  • Electrochemistry and Surface Chemistry
  • Coordination Compounds and d-block Elements

Biology

  • Human Physiology (Digestive, Respiratory, Circulatory)
  • Plant Physiology and Reproduction
  • Genetics and Evolution

Key Milestones:

  • First full-length NEET mock test
  • Subject-wise improvement assessment
  • Identification of weak areas for targeted improvement

Phase 3: Advanced Preparation (Months 7-9)

Physics

  • Magnetism and Electromagnetic Induction
  • Alternating Current and Electromagnetic Waves
  • Advanced Modern Physics

Chemistry

  • Advanced Organic Chemistry and Mechanisms
  • Biomolecules and Polymers
  • Chemistry in Everyday Life

Biology

  • Human Physiology (Nervous, Excretory, Reproductive)
  • Ecology and Environment
  • Biotechnology and Its Applications

Key Milestones:

  • Regular NEET pattern tests (bi-weekly)
  • Previous years' question paper practice
  • Comprehensive subject revision tests

All Subjects
